## Comments are lines that start with a '#'
## Any number of hashtags work
## Comments end at a newline
## All symbols can be escaped with a backslash '\' such as \# to have the symbol show up in the credits
## Headers are lines that start with a '>'
## Empty lines will be added as vertical padding
